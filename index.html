Questions

Frequently Asked

I'm a high school thrower outside San Diego. Can I train with you?

+

Due to NCAA compliance rules, I cannot provide in-person throws coaching to high school athletes outside the 50-mile radius of UC San Diego. However, I can offer remote programming and video analysis services, which are not subject to the same geographic restrictions.

I'm a college athlete at another university. Is this allowed?

+

Yes. Current college athletes from any institution can train with Podium Athletics. Since you're already enrolled, you're no longer considered a "prospective student-athlete" under NCAA rules. You're responsible for ensuring any training expenses comply with your institution's policies.

How do I know if I'm within the 50-mile radius?

+

The radius is measured from UC San Diego's campus in La Jolla, California. This generally includes most of San Diego County and southern portions of Orange County. Contact us with your home address (city) and we can verify eligibility.

What about NIL deals?

+

Training services must be paid at fair market value. Podium Athletics does not engage in NIL agreements tied to recruiting inducements or institutional affiliation. All coaching is provided as a professional service at standard industry rates.

Do you offer discounts for talented athletes?

+

No. All athletes are charged the same fair market rates regardless of ability level. Providing discounted rates based on athletic ability would violate NCAA amateurism rules and compromise your eligibility.
