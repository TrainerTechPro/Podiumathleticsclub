REGULATIONS

NCAA Compliance

Institutional Disclosure

Coach Sommers reports all outside coaching income to UC San Diego as required by NCAA Bylaw 11.2.2. All business activities receive proper institutional approval and oversight.

High School Athletes (Prospective Student-Athletes)

Due to NCAA recruiting regulations (Bylaw 13.11.2.4), we can only provide track & field throws training to high school athletes who are legal residents within 50 miles of UC San Diego's campus in La Jolla, California.

CRITICAL: This 50-mile radius requirement ensures full compliance with NCAA local sports club regulations and protects both athlete and institution.

Current College Athletes

Athletes from all NCAA institutions are welcome. Training services provided at fair market rates and not affiliated with recruiting inducements or institutional incentives. Athletes responsible for ensuring expenses comply with their institution's policies.

Fair Market Value & No Pay-for-Play

All coaching services priced at rates commensurate with the going rate in San Diego for similar professional services. Training fees are for coaching services only and not tied to athletic performance, recruiting decisions, or institutional affiliation.

QUESTIONS

FAQ

I'm a high school thrower outside San Diego. Can I train with you?

A: Due to NCAA compliance rules, I cannot provide in-person throws coaching to high school athletes outside the 50-mile radius of UC San Diego. However, I can offer remote programming and video analysis services, which are not subject to the same geographic restrictions.

I'm a college athlete at another university. Is this allowed?

A: Yes. Current college athletes from any institution can train with Podium Athletics. Since you're already enrolled, you're no longer considered a "prospective student-athlete" under NCAA rules. You're responsible for ensuring any training expenses comply with your institution's policies.

How do I know if I'm within the 50-mile radius?

A: The radius is measured from UC San Diego's campus in La Jolla, California. This generally includes most of San Diego County and southern portions of Orange County. Contact us with your home address (city) and we can verify eligibility.

What about NIL deals?

A: Training services must be paid at fair market value. Podium Athletics does not engage in NIL agreements tied to recruiting inducements or institutional affiliation. All coaching is provided as a professional service at standard industry rates.

Can I train during my high school season?

A: Yes, if you meet the 50-mile residency requirement. Training during your season is permissible under NCAA rules. Many athletes supplement their high school coaching with specialized technical work.

Do you offer discounts for talented athletes?

A: No. All athletes are charged the same fair market rates regardless of ability level. Providing discounted rates based on athletic ability would violate NCAA amateurism rules and compromise your eligibility.
